Murrow's Sustainability Club

Our school happens to have a Sustainability Club and I decided to join in my sophomore year. This year, my friend Eva and I have the pleasure to be co-presidents of the Club. We incorporate our school's greenhouse as part of our club activities. As members of the club, we try to raise awareness about environmental issues in our school community and educate students on how to be more sustainable in a school environment!
